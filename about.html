About Me

Capturing life's moments through my lens

Hello, I'm Nobz

I'm a passionate photographer based in the heart of the city, dedicated to capturing the authentic moments that make life extraordinary. Through my lens, I explore the intersection of urban life, natural beauty, and human emotion.

Photography has been my companion for over 8 years, taking me from quiet street corners to bustling markets, from intimate portraits to sweeping landscapes. Each image tells a story, and I believe in the power of visual storytelling to connect us all.

My Photography Journey

2016 - The Beginning

Started with a simple point-and-shoot camera, discovering the magic of capturing everyday moments.

2018 - First Online Gallery

Showcased my work on an online gallery, focusing on urban street photography and city life.

2020 - Digital Transition

Embraced digital photography and launched this blog to share stories behind each photograph.

2025 - Present

Continuing to explore new perspectives and techniques while building a community of photography enthusiasts.
